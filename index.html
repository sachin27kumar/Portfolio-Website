About Me

Passionate B.Tech graduate embracing web development.

I'm Sachin, a graduate with a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Deen Bandhu Chotu Ram University of Science and Technology, Sonipat, Haryana. Following my graduation, I embarked on a journey aimed at competitive exams such as SSC and CDS. However, my path took a turn when I discovered a passion for the IT sector and decided to shift my career focus towards web development.

Driven by a fervent interest in learning new technologies and engaging in creative design and development work, I delved into the world of web development.

Along the way, I gained valuable experience through internships in full-stack web development with various companies. Currently, I'm actively pursuing the Internshala Full Stack Web Development course, eager to expand my skills and expertise in this dynamic field.
